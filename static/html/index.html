      Our Love story

      Sept, 1999

      The Beginning

      David and Duong ran into each other in the fall of 1999 at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), where they both went to college. Duong was a freshman at UCSD and David was a transfer student. David went to high school and was friends with Duong's older sister Trang, so they knew of each other. When Duong saw David in the quad coming out of the Sunshine Store, she ran up to him, hugged him and invited him to stop by her apartment at K-15 as the door was always open. David accepted the invitation and always stopped by to hang out with Duong and/or her awesome roommates. Their friendship grew through college and David became one of Duong's best guy friends.

      Aug, 2004

      Duong moves to Michigan

      Duong moved to Michigan in the summer of 2004 to attend the University of Michigan, College of Pharmacy. GO BLUE! She continued to catch up with David when she came home to visit.

      May, 2007

      Serendipity

      Duong came home to San Diego for one of her sister's weddings and as she always did, called David to hang out. It was during that phone call that David shared with Duong that just the day before he had become a card carrying member of club single. They weren't able to meet up for lunch as they usually did, however David promised he'd visit Duong in Michigan. From that day forward Duong and David spoke on the phone regularly, just like the good ol' days at UCSD.

      Oct, 2007

      The First Kiss

      David came out to visit Duong for her birthday weekend in Ann Arbor, MI. They went out for dinner and drinks with Duong's friends to celebrate her birthday. The following day, they tailgated and attended a Michigan football game at the Big house (GO BLUE!). The fun continued with going to a German bar, the Hiedelberg, with a group of friends. All attendees had their motor skills fine-tuned by consuming several boots of beer. It was at the Heidelberg Bar in Ann Arbor where both Duong and David had a moment and they kissed. The kiss lead them to talk about their years of friendship and when the weekend ended and David went back to San Diego, they decided that they would take a chance and take their friendship to the next level.

      Aug, 2008

      Move to NJ

      Duong moved to New Jersey for a 2 year post-doctoral fellowship program after graduation. The long distance was getting difficult, thus David decided to quit his job, pack his car, and drive across the country to Jersey so that they could be closer.

      Apr 19, 2013

      The Proposal

      Duong and David went home to San Diego to visit their friends and family. They had both heard from friends how much UCSD had changed, so David suggested they go back to visit. Duong unaware of the true reason for the UCSD visit was demostrating her legendary patience by asking David why they were just hanging around the Price Center when David began: "I remember when I originally saw you I didn't say much because I was so surprised. What I should have said was 'Nine years from now I'm going to visit you in Michigan and you're going do something that begins our journey together. Five years after that I'm going to bring you back to this spot and do something to make sure that journey never ends.'." David got on one knee and proposed to Duong at the same location where they ran into each other at UCSD in 1999, where it all began.
